"Hello World" - this phrase used to be a phrase of curiority (you were likely learning something) and opportunity. Tech used to feel like that. In the last few years, that's changed and now tech feels... I'll say it, just gross. In 2026, there are already been so many layoffs, people seem mean and cruel, and I feel for so many people put in really difficult situations. It makes me feel a lot of anxiety, and I don't know if it will ever get better. I also have a lot to be grateful for, I am in a better position than most and have been given lots of opportunities that others don't get, so I feel grateful. But more and more, the feeling of gratitude is rooted in "it could be worse", and that pretty much defeats the purpose.
