We can't talk honestly about AI at work, and it's exhausting. We all should feel conflicted about AI. It's displacing jobs. It concentrates power to a small group of people. You can go on and on. There are real problems and we can't talk about it. We just have to pretend like it's all exciting, we are all on top of the latest tech, and we can go on like this forever. Just go look at the cesspool that is LinkedIn. I'm exhausted of the fact that we can't talk honestly about it. We all gotta pay those bills.
